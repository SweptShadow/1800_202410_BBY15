About Us

We are a team of dedicated BCIT students passionate about making daily commutes more manageable. Our application, Bus With Us, is designed to enhance the public transit experience by providing easy to access bus occupancy information. Utilizing a color-coded map and existing data, we aim to empower commuters with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions. Additionally, users can contribute to the accuracy of our database by sharing their own occupancy observations. Together, we're driving towards a future where public transportation is more accessible and efficient for everyone.
